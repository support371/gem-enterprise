# Owner Evidence Checklist

Use this checklist to collect the evidence required for public-claim approval. Do not commit confidential evidence, credentials, identity documents, or provider secrets to this public repository.

## Leadership and identity

- Approved legal/public name
- Role and effective date
- Consent to publish
- Approved biography
- Employment-history evidence
- Academic-credential evidence
- Professional-license or certification evidence
- Publication restrictions or expiry dates

## Staffing and coverage

- Current team roster by function
- Employment or contractor status
- Shift and escalation schedule
- Geographic coverage evidence
- After-hours coverage evidence
- Support operating hours

## Service performance

- Source data for acknowledgement and response-time claims
- Measurement period
- Sample size
- Exclusions
- Approved public statistic
- Review and expiry date

## Providers and integrations

- Provider legal name
- Contract or authorization status
- Approved service scope
- Data source or integration scope
- Production, sandbox, planned, or disabled state
- Public naming permission

## Memberships and partnerships

- Organization name
- Membership or partnership type
- Effective date
- Expiration date
- Permission to display name or logo
- Approved wording

## Security and compliance

- Encryption architecture summary
- Independent assessment evidence
- Compliance framework scope
- Certification scope and issuer
- Audit or assessment date
- Approved limitations and disclaimers

## Products and pricing

- Product owner
- Product availability state
- Supported jurisdiction
- Provider dependencies
- Pricing authority
- Indicative or binding price state
- Fulfillment readiness
- Refund and cancellation policy state

## Storage rule

Store confidential evidence in an approved private evidence vault. Record only a non-secret evidence identifier, owner, review status, effective date, and expiry date in the application or repository.
