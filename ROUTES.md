# GEM Enterprise Route Map

## Public
Includes Home, Intel, Services, Community, Hub, Resources, Company, Contact, Get Started, KYC, decisions, and legal pages as required in `src/lib/platform.ts`.

## Protected
`/app` surface includes dashboard, products, portfolios, documents, support, compliance, requests, profile, settings, security, notifications, messages, and admin routes.

Access bridge: `/access/continue` applies session + KYC + entitlement routing logic.
